What About House Fires Are Important to Know?

House fires could happen to anyone, anywhere, and at anytime. These house fires could happen much too easily and much too often.
Many people either don't know about the methods that would keep them safe from a house fire, or they don't care to practice them.
This might then ultimately lead to these people risking a fire starting in their houses.



How Does the House Fire Prevention Website Educate the Public?

House fires could occur much less if the general public were educated the its dangers and its preventions. This website was created in order to
educate people on the dangers of house fires and how to prevent them.



What Is Our Mission?

We aim to educate the public as to lessen the amount of house fires. Therefore, decreasing the amount of lives affected by domestic fires.
Hundreds to thousands of homes could have a decreased possibility of catching fire just by simply educating ourselves on this topic.
